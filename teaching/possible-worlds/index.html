Further reading

  • Pretty much any basic textbook on probability or statistics.
  • Seeing Theory (Brown University) - an interactive probability textbook in the same spirit; the compound probability and Bayesian inference chapters pick up where this page leaves off, in particular, for conditional probability.
  • 3Blue1Brown, "Bayes theorem" - the geometric intuition behind the posterior bars, beautifully animated.
  • Jaynes, Probability Theory: The Logic of Science, chapters 1-2 - for the ambitious: probability as the logic of plausible reasoning, where hypotheses live in the mind of the reasoner.
