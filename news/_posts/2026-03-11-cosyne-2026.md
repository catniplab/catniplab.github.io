[COSYNE 2026](https://www.cosyne.org/) is in Lisbon this year (March 12–15 main meeting, March 16–17 workshops in Cascais). #cosyne2026

## CATNIPLAB at COSYNE 2026

We have three posters and a workshop talk this year!

### Posters

- **[2-152] Dynamical archetype analysis: Autonomous computation** [(arXiv)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.05505)
  Ábel Ságodi, Il Memming Park
  *(Friday, Poster Session 2)*
  A robust dissimilarity measure for grouping dynamical systems by their asymptotic behavior, giving a vocabulary of archetypes for neural computation.

- **[3-213] Symmetry-Regularized Learning of Continuous Attractor Dynamics**
  Arthur Liang, Ábel Ságodi, Piotr Sokół, Il Memming Park
  *(Saturday, Poster Session 3)*
  Embedding rotational symmetry priors into latent dynamical models yields parsimonious, interpretable ring-attractor fits without sacrificing predictive power.

- **[3-223] Tracking Changes in Neural Dynamics over the Course of Learning**
  Justus Kautz, Ayesha Vermani, Il Memming Park
  *(Saturday, Poster Session 3)*
  A meta-dynamical framework that tracks how population dynamics evolve over learning, revealing emergence of distinct computational strategies from activity alone.
